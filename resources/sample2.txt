Abdul Rashid Salim Salman Khan, born on 27 December 1965 is an Indian actor, film producer, and television personality who predominantly works in Hindi films. In a career spanning over three decades, his awards include two National Film Awards as a film producer, and two Filmfare Awards as an actor. He has been cited in the media as one of the most popular and commercially successful actors of Indian cinema. Forbes included him in listings of the highest-paid celebrities in the world, in 2015 and 2018. He has starred in the annual highest-grossing Hindi films of 10 individual years, the highest for any actor.
